Chef Ann Willie Eti

Executive Chef · Culinary Instructor · Communications Artist

Chef Ann Willie Eti is an accomplished Executive Chef, Culinary Instructor, and Communications Artist with a passion for excellence in food, education, and professional storytelling.

With extensive experience in culinary operations, menu development, kitchen leadership, hospitality training, and communication strategy, she combines technical expertise with creativity to deliver exceptional results.

Her mission is to inspire, educate, and create unforgettable culinary experiences while mentoring future industry professionals.
