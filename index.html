About ChefAnn

A Career Built On Craft, Discipline & Purpose

ChefAnn Willie Eti is an accomplished Executive Chef, Culinary Instructor, Mentor and Communications Professional dedicated to transforming lives through culinary excellence, leadership and practical education.

Through years of experience, she has trained aspiring chefs, empowered women through professional culinary programs, mentored hospitality professionals and inspired excellence across communities. Her approach pairs technical precision with genuine care for the people she teaches, treating every kitchen as a classroom and every classroom as the start of a career.

Guiding Principles

Mission, Vision & Core Values

Mission

To equip aspiring chefs and hospitality professionals, especially women, with the technical skill, confidence and leadership needed to build lasting careers in culinary arts.

Vision

A hospitality industry where every kitchen reflects excellence, every chef leads with integrity, and opportunity is accessible to anyone willing to learn the craft.

Core Values

Excellence, discipline, mentorship and empowerment. Every program is built on practical skill, honest feedback and respect for the people we train.
